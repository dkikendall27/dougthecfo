About

Professional,
strategic & human

I am a finance executive based in the Greater Seattle region with over 19 years of experience, starting my career at Moss Adams in 2007. I partner with founders and leadership teams through growth, operational scale, capital raises, and M&A.

I bring a practical, hands-on approach, connecting financial insight to operational clarity so companies can execute with confidence and scale with discipline. I believe great work happens when strong relationships, intellectual curiosity, and a little personality are part of the process.

Outside of work, I am a husband and dad to two kids who keep life wonderfully unpredictable. My wife and I are raising a household full of energy, laughter, and the occasional controlled chaos that comes with parenthood. When I am not at the whiteboard or deep in a model, you will find me chasing live music across the Pacific Northwest or planning the next road trip somewhere worth the drive.
